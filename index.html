My life

I was born on a cold night in the winter of 2015. Me and my 7 siblings were happy little pups until that one day. But never mind... my tragic backstory is way too epic for you peasants. [...]

Soon I was adopted by the Rads. They were hoping I was black, much to their surprise I AM NOT. How dare they be racist. Just because my siblings were black, doesn't mean I am!

However I forced them to like me. I mean... I came potty trained! What more do you expect? I even ate my food quickly without having them beg me. But that came to an end. Of course they'd have to beg me. Even their daughter had to beg me to fetch the ball, as if I didn't know how.

My god I am awesome. I didn't even make this website. They made it for me. That's how much they adore me.
